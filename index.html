La Dolce Vita Records


Coming out in Winter

Welcome to La Dolce Vita Records, where Cape Town's rhythm meets the heartbeat of music. Founded by the dynamic duo Sheldon and Hassan, our label is more than a name – it's a celebration of sound, a fusion of passion and creativity.

Situated in the vibrant cultural landscape of Cape Town, La Dolce Vita Records is a testament to the rich diversity of musical expressions. We curate a collection transcending genres, capturing the city's soul in every note.

At La Dolce Vita Records, we believe in the timeless allure of vinyl – the warmth, the crackle, the authenticity. Our mission is to bring you music that resonates in your ears and lingers in your soul.

We're not just a record label; we're curators of sonic experiences, weaving stories through melodies and etching memories into vinyl grooves.

Our founders, Sheldon and Hassan, bring a wealth of experience and a shared love for boundaryless music. Join us on this musical odyssey through the eclectic soundscape of Cape Town. La Dolce Vita Records – where the rhythm never stops, and the music speaks louder than words.
